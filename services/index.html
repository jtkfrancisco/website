1. Conversation
In our initial conversation, I’ll review your project scope and ask you a number of questions to get a sense of what you are looking for and what I can provide to you. Once we’ve agreed to work together, we will finalize the details and when a deposit of 50% is made, we will begin working on your project.
2. Research
I'll ask you for inspiration pictures to get a feel for the kind of imagery you want me to work with. I will also be conducting my own research on any other areas that could be of interest for the design process.
3. Development
I will put together some initial sketches and designs conveying the concepts that we have come up with for the project. Once the direction is approved by you, I begin the process of refinement.
4. Refinement
Having created designs for you to review, during this stage I will be seeking your feedback. I will prompt you with specific questions to make sure the creative executions are meeting the needs of your specific business communication goals.
5. Completion
Once we’ve completed necessary revisions, we will tie up loose ends and release your vision into the world. You will be invoiced for your remaining balance and as soon as the final payment is received, I’ll send you a download link to access your files.
