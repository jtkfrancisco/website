I am Justin Francisco, a Toronto-based graphic designer and visual artist. My training as a graphic designer comes from the Ontario College of Art and Design University (( OCAD U )) and working one on one with professional clients.

I design because I am passionate about communicating messages and invoking experiences through images and video.

My specialization is in high quality logo design, but I strive to be the designer that can approach any problem
with confidence. It is my ethos to be always learning new skills and methods for visual commun-ication, so that I can either handle the task at hand with the knowledge I already have, or be able to efficiently learn new things to aid me in solving a given problem.

I want to work with you to properly communicate your message.
